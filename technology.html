Our Proprietary INDRA Technology

At KO Energy, we develop high-performance INDRA micro wind turbines, engineered for zero gear-related wear, noiseless operation, and seamless hybrid integration.

INDRA Series Specifications

  • INDRA-I (1 kW): 72 kg | Rotor: 10 ft | Daily Output: 5 kWh (at 5.5 m/s) | Systems: 24/48/230V
  • INDRA-III (3 kW): 90 kg | Rotor: 14 ft | Daily Output: 15 kWh (at 5.5 m/s) | Systems: 48/230V
  • INDRA-V (5 kW): 100 kg | Rotor: 16 ft | Daily Output: 25 kWh (at 5.5 m/s) | Systems: 48/230V
  • Start-up: Begins generating in a gentle 1.4 m/s breeze.
  • Survival: Built to withstand cyclonic winds up to 60 m/s (134 mph).
  • Versatility: Built to perform in diverse weather ranging from marine and desert to snow.

Innovative Features

  • Axial Flux Architecture: Multi-phase, multi-voltage, brushless, and super efficient.
  • Direct Drive and Gear-less: Zero gear-related wear and tear or oil changes.
  • Noiseless Profile: Less than 10% of the sound of the wind itself at foundation level.
  • RPM Control: Automatically faces any wind direction for longer bearing life and safe rooftop installation.
  • Bird-Friendly: Daytime visibility paint on blades and night-time glow rings.
  • Efficiency: Requires only 1/3rd the space of solar panels for equivalent output.

AURA: The Story and the Tech

The word AURA refers to divine energy or a halo, and in Greek mythology, it is the name of the goddess of breeze. Its biomimetic design is inspired by TETHYS, the moon of Saturn, manifesting as an elegant LED light ring around the unit.

AURA is the world's first wall-mounted 4-in-1 Clean Energy Inverter (PCU). It seamlessly integrates wind, solar, and storage, creating a stable, self-sufficient local energy system available in cosmic-themed colours like Celestial White, Galaxy Blue, and Aurora Green.
