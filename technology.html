Our Proprietary INDRA Technology

At KO Energy, we develop high-performance INDRA micro wind turbines, engineered for zero gear-related wear, noiseless operation, and seamless hybrid integration.

INDRA Series Specifications

  • INDRA-I (1 kW): 72 kg | Ideal for households below 200kWh/month.
  • INDRA-III (3 kW): 90 kg | Suitable for consumption up to 450kWh/month.
  • INDRA-V (5 kW): 100 kg | For higher energy needs up to 750kWh/month.
  • Energy Yield: Up to 5 kWh/day for INDRA-I (at 5.5 m/s).
  • Start-up: Begins generating in a gentle 1.4 m/s breeze.
  • Survival: Built to withstand cyclonic winds up to 60 m/s (134 mph).

Innovative Features

  • Axial Flux Architecture: Multi-phase, brushless, and super efficient.
  • Gear-less Direct Drive: Zero gear-related wear or oil changes.
  • Noiseless Profile: Less than 10% of the sound of the wind itself.
  • RPM Control: Longer bearing life and safe for residential rooftops.
  • Bird-Friendly: Visibility paint and night-time glow rings.
  • 20-Year Life: Designed for long-term reliability and low maintenance.

AURA: The Ultimate Hybrid Solution

Introducing AURA, our wall-mounted 4-in-1 clean energy inverter. Engineered to blend wind, solar, and storage, AURA creates a stable, self-sufficient local energy system for your home or farm.
