KO Energy delivers tailored micro wind and hybrid systems built on our advanced technology, designed to eliminate energy poverty and create self-sufficiency for homes, farms, and businesses.

Target Applications

Residential & Home

Power your entire home with our compact, noiseless INDRA turbines. Occupying only 1/3rd the space of solar, they are perfect for rooftop installations.

Agriculture & Farms

Achieve energy independence for remote farm operations, irrigation systems, and livestock facilities with robust, all-weather generation.

Commercial & Industrial

Scalable onsite generation for warehouses, factories, and campuses seeking to meet ESG goals and improve energy resilience.

  1. Consultation: We analyze your site, energy loads, and long-term objectives.
